Volunteer in various agencies

Frontier Kenya

Frontier offers volunteer programs in Kenya that focus on wildlife conservation, marine conservation, and community development. Volunteers have the opportunity to contribute to vital conservation efforts and community projects in Kenya.

Africa Impact

African Impact operates volunteer projects across Africa, including Kenya. Their Kenya projects include wildlife conservation, teaching, sports development, and community health initiatives. Volunteers work alongside local communities for sustainable change.

IVHQ (International Volunteer HQ)

IVHQ offers affordable volunteer programs in Kenya, with options such as teaching, childcare, wildlife conservation, and healthcare. Volunteers have the opportunity to immerse themselves in Kenyan culture while making a positive impact.

Love Volunteers

Love Volunteers provides volunteer opportunities in Kenya focusing on areas such as orphanage support, teaching, healthcare, and community development. They aim to create sustainable change through meaningful volunteer work.

Go Volunteer Africa

Go Volunteer Africa offers a range of volunteer programs in Kenya, including teaching, healthcare, orphanage support, women's empowerment, and wildlife conservation. They emphasize responsible volunteering and cultural immersion.

GVI (Global Vision International)

GVI operates volunteer projects worldwide, including in Kenya. Their Kenya programs focus on wildlife conservation, community development, teaching, and healthcare. Volunteers work alongside local organizations to support long-term sustainable initiatives.
